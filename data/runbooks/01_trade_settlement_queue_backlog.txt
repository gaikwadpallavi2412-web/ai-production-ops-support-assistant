Title: Trade Settlement Queue Backlog Handling
Service: trade-settlement
Severity: High
Owner Team: Trade-Ops

Symptoms:

Settlement queue depth increasing rapidly

Trades stuck in PENDING status

Downstream confirmations delayed

MQ queue showing sustained backlog

Initial Checks:

Check MQ queue depth metrics

Verify settlement consumer pod health

Review DB latency and locks

Check recent deployment changes

Resolution Steps:

Restart settlement consumer pods

Validate and reprocess stuck messages

Scale consumer deployment if backlog persists

Monitor queue drain rate

Escalation:
Page Trade-Ops on-call if backlog persists beyond 30 minutes.
