Incident ID: INC-001
Service: trade-settlement
Severity: P1
Region: EU

Summary:
Trade settlement delays observed across EU region with queue backlog exceeding threshold.

Impact:
Approximately 18% of trades delayed.

Root Cause:
Settlement consumer pods experienced memory spike and restarted.

Resolution:
Pods restarted and HPA limits increased.

Duration:
38 minutes

Postmortem Notes:
Recommend memory tuning and alert threshold review.
