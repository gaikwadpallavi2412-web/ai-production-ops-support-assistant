Title: Users Reporting Payment Timeouts

Priority: High
Status: Resolved
Assigned Group: Payments-Ops

User Description:

Impact:
Increased payment retries and minor customer complaints.

Investigation:

Upstream bank latency high

Gateway thread pool near saturation

Increased thread pool size

Enabled retry backoff

Upstream latency combined with insufficient thread capacity.

Validation:
Timeout rate dropped below 0.5%.
